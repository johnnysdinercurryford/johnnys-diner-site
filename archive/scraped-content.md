### About / Story
Johnny's Diner on Curry Ford has been family owned & operated since 1995. Started as a humble 8-booth diner. Expanded alongside its neighborhood. Known for homestyle cooking — hot off the flat-top, with friendly faces and a fresh pot of coffee. Specializes in buttermilk pancakes, classic omelets, hand-formed burgers, and stacked sandwiches. Proud Member of the Curry Ford West Community. Partners with area farms and bakeries.
